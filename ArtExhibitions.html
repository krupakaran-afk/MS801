Galway International Arts Festival

“The Chaser” Basil Joseph Blackshaw

At the Galway International Arts Festival event, we are displaying the beautiful works of Basil Joseph Blackshaw.

The Linen Hall Library issued the Chaser by Basil Blackshaw as a full-colour reproduction print on 135 gsm paper. The image size is slightly reduced from the original, executed in 1992 and a Private collection. Register now!

Kilkenny Arts Festival

"Liffey Swim" Jack Butler Yeats

At the Kilkenny Arts Festival event, we are displaying the beautiful works of Jack Butler Yeats.

The artistic significance of the Liffey Swim in Irish life is emphasised by the Jack B Yeats painting of the same name. The Irish Free State went on to win the first Olympic medal for swimming in 1924 in Paris (Liam Ryan, 2019). Register now!
