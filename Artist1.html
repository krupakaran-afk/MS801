Gallery

Six Mile Valley

Six mile valley is an area in County Antrim close to where Basil Blackshaw lived and pained for many years. This painting captures the sense of scale and expansivenes where Blackshaw seems to be drawn to unpopulated landscapes.

Three Goats

This painting depicts how Blackshaw evokes a sense of physical solidity with a minimum of means.

Horse and Cart

This painting dates back to early 1990s. It depicts Blackshaw's distinctly sensitivity in the handling of colour and painterly bravura of an experienced artist.

Professor Sir David Bates

David Bates, a mathematician and theoretical physicist, received his education at the Royal Belfast Academical Institution, Queen's University, and University College, London, where he became a lecturer and reader in 1951.

Racehorses Excercising

This painting has Blackshaw's gentler outlook with soft tonal colours emphasising the significance of harmony.

Anna on a Sofa

'Anna on a Sofa' deviates from Blackshaw's usual style. It is built with fewer materials. The canvas has been used less extensively in terms of color and composition than is typical of his painting style.
