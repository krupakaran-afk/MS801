About Us

The Prism Art Gallery began as a summer project for the Irish Arts Foundation and has since grown into a full-time business that welcomes visitors all year. Today, the gallery has a long-standing reputation in the west of Ireland for promoting fine art painting of the greatest quality.

Prism Art Gallery opened in 2007 in Galway, Galway is known as Ireland's festival capital, hosting an average of 122 festivals and events each year

In addition to representing international artists, the gallery dedicates ample space for the promotion of Irish artists both abroad and in the galway city, giving a strong focus to young emerging talent
