Prism Garden Café

Visit our beautiful Garden Café for breakfast and lunch. Call us on 0214 12345 for reservations.

Sinead Doran runs the PRISM Art Gallery Café, a unique institution located on the ground level of Crawford Art Gallery. Mrs Myrtle Allen and her daughter Fern first opened the doors of the PRISM Art Gallery Cafe in 1986. The cafe's new iteration maintains the Ballymaloe ideal of taking the highest quality food and cooking it simply yet attractively.
