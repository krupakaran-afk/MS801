Stream of Splendour

Basil Joseph Blackshaw

(1932-2016)

About Me

Basil Blackshaw is a renowned Irish painter of his generation. Blackshaw’s work is known by the use of distinctive colours and loose application of paint often dominated by his post expressionist landscape paintings, animal paintings.

Jack Butler Yeats

(1871-1957)

About Me

Jack B. Yeats is one of Ireland’s most significant artists. Yeats began his artistic career as a black and whole journalist illustrator for various magazines. His early paintings depicts of taking a realistic approach to images of urban and rural life.

Andrew Nicholl

(1804-1886)

About Me

Andrew Nicholl is a nineteenth century artist known for his Irish landscapes favouring watercolours and is a draughtsman illustrator. He is founder member of the Belfast Association of Artists.
